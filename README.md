Simulated Trading Platform

Virtual stock trading environment using real-life comapnies listed on the London Stock Exchange.
Users can buy/sell stock shares with virtual currency, and can take out virtual 'loans' if they become low on funds. 

The program takes real-life stock prices of companies from the London Stock Exchange Website, but has additioanl algorithms implemented to adjust the prices of stock shares at random intervals so users can still trade outside of market hours.
